Our values

Human Connection

We believe that genuine human connection is what brings joy and meaning to life. Our mission is to create opportunities for people to connect with each other and form lasting bonds. Nothing beats a meaningful connection with someone else.

Social Experience

We believe that life is best experienced together. Whether it’s through shared moments with friend or new experiences with strangers. We're dedicated to creating opportunities for people to come together and enjoy each other's company.

Humor

We're serious about not being too serious, and we're on a mission to spread joy and laughter wherever we go, through clever quips or with a playful approach. Our brand is playful, witty, and we're always looking for new ways to make you smile.
